ABOUT

The EcoChain project revolves around the $EcoChain token and improving the environment. Many people would be perplexed as to how a blockchain-based cryptocurrency can help the environment, but one project has done so in the simplest yet most effective way conceivable.

A percentage of the tokens' proceeds will go toward planting new trees. Users of the $EcoChain Token will be able to select where the tree from their money will be planted. As a result, people will feel as if they are contributing to the planet's survival.

By planting trees and forestation, this effort will help to solve some of the world's most serious concerns.
